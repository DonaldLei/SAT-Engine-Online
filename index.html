Our Mission

Our website serves as a progress tracker that fills in the gaps in students’ skills through assessing their weaknesses from diagnostics, and correcting them through targeted practice.

The hours where students are not in tutoring sessions are crucial in maintaining students’ consistency and addressing any issues that they may have. However, these issues that surface cannot be immediately addressed because of the time that tutors need to identify them. Through our website, it will immediately identify and address issues that students may have before their tutor sessions.
